New features: formatting entries
 

Text in title case
When displaying entries in formatted view, Cindex can now format headings and cross-references in title case (the initial letter of each word is capitalized, with some exceptions). This is available as a new option in the Style settings you make through the Headings… and Cross-References… items in the Document menu.

Page references
When displaying a fully formatted index, Cindex much more effectively detects and automatically suppresses page references and ranges that overlap redundantly.
