Keyboard Shortcuts

 

You can use your keyboard to conveniently accomplish many tasks in Cindex. To find the shortcuts for common commands, look in the menus. Several items in Cindex (for example, the main index window and the record-entry window) also have contextual menus. To see a contextual menu, right-click the item or press the Control key and click the item.

Some actions have shortcut keys that are not in menus. These are listed in the table below.
