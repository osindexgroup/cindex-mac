Managing cross-references
 

Cindex provides two powerful tools for managing cross-references:

•

Cindex can generate cross-references automatically, so that the existence of a specified entry can lead automatically to the creation of an appropriate cross-reference. How to generate cross-references.

•

If a cross reference points to to few target entries, Cindex can automatically convert the cross reference to a fully qualified entry. How to convert cross-references.
