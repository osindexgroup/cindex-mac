Using the Spelling panel
 

Use the Spelling panel (Edit menu) to check the spelling of words in records.

To begin checking the spelling of entries click Start. Cindex will examine entries from the first accessible in the current view.

If Cindex finds a word it does not know, a questionably punctuated word, or a duplicated word, it will display the word in red at the top right-hand corner of the panel, and may also display a copy for correction.

To correct the word, type in the text box, or click a word in the list of suggestions, then click Change.

To ignore a word, click Ignore; to ignore all occurrences of the word, click Ignore All.

To add a word to a personal dictionary that Cindex will use subsequently, click Add. If no personal dictionary is currently in use, Cindex will ask you to specify one.

You can control which records are examined, as follows:

•

To search among a subset of displayed records specify the scope of the search.

•

To confine the search to new records, check New Records; to confine the search to records modified in your current session check Modified Records.

•

To confine the search to specified levels of heading, choose from the Field popup menu.

•

Cindex normally checks only cross-references in the locator field. If you want Cindex to examine the spelling of locators other than cross-references, check Check Page Refs.
