Hiding records
 

When viewing a fully-formatted index you can control the visibility of records according to the types of labels that they carry. You can hide records that have any label (or no label).

To specify which records should be hidden choose Hide by Attribute… from the View menu. In the panel Cindex displays check the labels for which you want to hide records. Also check Enable Hiding.

Records that are hidden are invisible to all commands that act globally on the index, except Compress and Expand, which ignore the Hide by Attribute… setting.
