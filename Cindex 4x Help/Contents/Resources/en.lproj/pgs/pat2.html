Sample patterns
 

The following patterns specify various kinds of text you might want to find.

•

[A–Za–z]+ Any word (a sequence of one or more letters). This set includes accented letters.

•

[1–9]+ Any simple page reference (a sequence of one or more numerals beginning with a numeral in the range 1-9).

•

[1–9]+–[1–9]+ Any reference to an inclusive range of pages connected by a hyphen (a sequence of one or more numerals beginning with a numeral in the range 1-9, followed by a hyphen, followed by another sequence of one or more numerals beginning with one in the range 1-9).

•

[ ][ ]+ Any sequence of two or more spaces.

•

([A–Za–z]+) +\1 Successive occurrences of the same word (one or more letters followed by one or more spaces followed by the original sequence of letters).

•

\(.+\) Any sequence of one or more characters enclosed in parentheses.

•

\.$ Any period at the end of a field.

•

^[^A–Za–z] Any character except a letter at the beginning of a field.

Cindex provides a rich set of ready-made patterns via the contextual menu displayed when you right-click the Find box in the Find or Replace Panel.
