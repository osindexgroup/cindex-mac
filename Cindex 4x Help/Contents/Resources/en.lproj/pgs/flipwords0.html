Managing Smart Flip Words
 

To specify the leading and trailing articles and prepositions that Cindex can move when exchanging (flipping) the contents of two record fields choose Smart Flip Words… from the Document menu.

If you place a ~ immediately before any word in the set, Cindex will move that word from one field to the other when flipping, but will not move it from the beginning to the end of the field, or vice-versa. Words for which you would typically want this behavior are "and" and "versus."

If you set Smart Flip words when no index is open for work, the settings become the default ones that Cindex establishes for new indexes when you create them.

To enable smart flipping, choose Preferences… (Cindex menu) and on the Editing tab check Smart Flip.
