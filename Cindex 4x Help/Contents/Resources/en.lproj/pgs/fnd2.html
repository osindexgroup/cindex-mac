Finding records with particular attributes
 

Use the Find panel (Edit menu) to search among records that have particular attributes or lack particular attributes.

In the Only Among box first choose whether to search among records that have all of the specified attributes, or have none of the specified attributes.

•

To search among records that are (or are not) new, check New Records (this is dimmed if there are no new records).

•

To search among records that are (or are not) modified, check Modified Records.

•

To search among records that are (or are not) deleted, check Deleted Records (this is dimmed if entries are displayed in a fully formatted view).

•

To search among records that are (or are not) marked, check Marked Records.

•

To search among records that are (or are not) generated, check Generated Records.

•

To search among records that are (or are not) labeled, check Labeled Records, and choose which label to search for. This item is dimmed if you are viewing a fully formatted index and have chosen Hide by Attribute… (View menu) to hide labeled records.

To search among records made or modified by a particular user, enter the user's identifying initials at UserID.

To find records with particular attributes, regardless of the text they contain, do not specify search text.
