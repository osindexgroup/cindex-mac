Finding text styles and fonts
 

Use the Find panel (Edit menu) to search among records for text with particular attributes.

To find records that contain (or do not contain) text formatted in a particular style and/or font, click Attributes… beside the Find box. Cindex will display a panel through which you can specify the attributes that must be carried (or must not be carried) by the text you are looking for.

Cindex can search for style and font attributes regardless of the text to which they are applied. To find any text set in a particular style or font, click Attributes… to specify the style and font you want, but do not specify search text.
