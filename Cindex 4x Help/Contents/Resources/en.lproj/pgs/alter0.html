Adjusting page references
 

Use the Alter References panel (Tools menu) to make adjustments to page numbers in index records.

Through this panel you can add offsets to page numbers or subtract offsets from them. For example, you can add 2 to every reference to pages between 550 and 634. You can also remove references to page numbers.

Before using the steps described in this section you should ensure that Cindex knows the character you use to denote an inclusive range of references, and to separate successive references when more than one appears in a page field.
