Editing an existing record
 

From the main view of the index you can edit any record by double-clicking it or by selecting it then hitting return or choosing Edit Record from the Edit menu. Cindex will display the record in the record window.

Using commands from the Edit menu, or from a contextual menu that Cindex displays when you click the right-hand mouse button in the record-entry window, you can:

  • Cut, copy and paste text.
  • Undo and redo changes.
  • Insert anywhere the text of a field from the last record you edited. To recall the main heading from the previously-edited record, hit CONTROL 1; to recall the subheading hit CONTROL 2. Use this principle to recall any subheading up to level 8. To recall the previous locator field, hit CONTROL 0.
  • Drag selected text around within the window, and into it from other programs. When you drag text out of a record entry window, Cindex always copies it. When you drag text within a record-entry window, Cindex moves it unless you hold down the CONTROL key, in which case Cindex copies the text. To cancel a drag in progress, hit ESC. If the text you have dragged originates outside Cindex, it will carry style information only if the other program supplies that information with the dragged text.

If you are pasting or dropping text that originates outside Cindex (e.g., from a PDF document), text you paste or drop retains font and style attributes according to your Preferences settings (retain both style and font information, retain only style information, or retain neither). As you paste or drop, you can override the default behavior as follows:

  • Hold the SHIFT key to deliver plain text (if the default behavior is to deliver with styles or styles & fonts) or to deliver text with styles and fonts (if the default behavior is to deliver plain text).
  • Hold the OPTION key to deliver styled text in the default font (if the default behavior is to deliver with styles & fonts or plain text) or to deliver text with styles and fonts (if the default behavior is to deliver styled text).

When pasting or dropping text that originates within Cindex, hold the OPTION key to discard font but not style information; hold the SHIFT key to discard font and style information.

To discard any changes you have made, and restore the record to its original state, click or hit OPTION ESC; to discard changes and close the window, hit ESC. When you have completed your changes, hit COMMAND RETURN to save the record and close the window, or hit PAGE UP or PAGE DOWN to save and move to the previous or next record. You can also use toolbar buttons in the record window.

To leave behind a copy of the edited record as the foundation of a new record, hit OPTION PAGE DOWN or click the appropriate toolbar button.

When you finish editing a series of records, Cindex normally leaves the main view window showing the last-edited record. If you prefer, You can make Cindex restore the display to its state before you started editing. To do that, choose Preferences… from the Cindex menu, then click the Editing tab and check Return to Entry Point.
