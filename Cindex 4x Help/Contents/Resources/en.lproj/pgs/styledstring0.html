Styling text automatically
 

Use the Styled Strings panel (Document menu) to specify text styles that should be applied automatically to specified words and phrases wherever they occur in the index..

You can always apply a style explicitly to text in individual records, but if you regularly use words or phrases that must be displayed in a particular style, you can save time and effort by entering them in records in plain text, and allowing Cindex to apply the style automatically when it displays or prints fully-formatted entries, in much the way it applies styles automatically to cross-references or page references.

To review or change the styles that will be applied to a particular word or phrase, click it to select it, then check or uncheck the appropriate boxes.

To edit the selected word or phrase, double-click it.

To delete the selected word or phrase, click -.

To add a new word or phrase to the list, click + then type it in the row that Cindex creates at the top of the table.

If you set Styled Strings when no index is open for work, the settings become the default ones that Cindex uses for new indexes when you create them.
